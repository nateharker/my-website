I Am Zambia Project


This project started as an assignement for my System Analysis and Design class for the IS program. We were supposed to find a system that could be improved in an organization then conduct a comprehensive analysis and create a formal proposal for improvements. We found a local non-profit, I Am Zambia, and saw many improvements that could be made to their volunteer onboarding process. We analyzed the current process and designed a new system that would be much simpler to use, while also saving them time and money. Though the project could have ended there, my group felt passionate enough about the cause that we decided to build a prototype recruiting website to show how the improvements we had proposed could be implemented.
