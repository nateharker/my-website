About Me

This is who I am.

My name is Nathan Harker. I live in Provo, Utah and am a senior at Brigham Young University. I am an Information Systems major in the Marriott School of Business and I will be starting an integrated Master of Information System Management program at BYU in the fall. Upon graduating from college, I will pursue a career in technology consulting and maybe later a career in machine learning or data analytics. I love playing all kinds of sports, but especially tennis and basketball. I am also an avid fan of the Boston Celtics and the Seattle Seahawks. I love to be outside, exercise, go on hikes, and travel as much as I can. I got married to the love of my life over two years ago and through careful budgeting, my wife and I have been able to visit Mexico, Portugal, France, Germany, Italy, Switzerland, and Hawaii.
